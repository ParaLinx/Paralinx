About Us

Three guys with the same obsession and love for technology. We like to know how everything works and how it is put together, that starts by taking it apart. Now that we know how it all works, we can fix it when it breaks. The satisfaction we get when we bring something broken back to life is well worth the time and effort.

After doing repairs for family members and seeing how proud of our work they were, something clicked. We talked about how cool it would be to begin doing various computer services not only as a hobby, but as a job. Weeks went by where it was all we talked about. Now, after long planning and hours of spit-balling ideas, that dream has become a reality. Three best friends fixing things together is just what Paralinx Technologies is all about.

The members of the Paralinx team are Brent Prox, Darrin Hiller, and Chris Palmer. All of us are pursuing various degrees in the technology field.

We do what we do because we love it. It isn't just a pay check, although that does help to pay the bills. We believe in giving all our customers quality products and service. When you speak to a previous customer of ours, we want them to tell you how outstanding our service is. That is why we do our best to ensure the customer's needs are met with the utmost quality service.

Paralinx Technologies will continue to grow into a massive company in the future. Since we offer such a wide range of services, we are versatile.

Be a part of the Paralinx evolution. We need you.
